At Cesar's Coffee Cup, we supply specialty-grade green beans to independent cafés, roasters, and coffee businesses across Melbourne and Australia. Our current selection covers origins from Colombia, Panama, Brazil, Ethiopia, Guatemala, and beyond — all traceable, quality-checked, and ready to roast.

Whether you're looking for a consistent commercial blend base, a traceable single origin for your espresso bar, or a rare lot to feature as a filter or specialty offering — we can supply the right green bean for your program.

Pair our wholesale green beans with our co-roasting service and we become your full-service coffee production partner. No unnecessary lock-in. No hidden costs. Just quality coffee, your way.
